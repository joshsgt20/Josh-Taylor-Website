Employment


BSH Home Appliances

Marketing Executive & Scrum Master

Currently, I work at BSH Home Appliances - a global company that houses four major household brands for the UK (Bosch, Siemens, NEFF and Gaggenau). I work in a high performing, agile team as a Digital Marketing Executive & Scrum Master - meaning I work daily on complex tasks whilst leading and supporting the rest of the team.

In this role, my focus frequently shifts across individual marketing and website touchpoints, brands and projects.

Team Leadership - Scrum Master

A Scrum Master is someone who works in a leadership role within an agile, 'Scrum' team. It is part of my responsibilty to support the team and keep work on track, ensuring it meets the full requirements and deadlines of everyone involved, whilst also helping the team develop both individually and collectively.

The role requires a large amount of communication with stakeholders, other teams and my own team, needing to ensure all potential blockers are resolved and that the team have all the confidence and resources to complete each task.

Applying this knowledge

The skills and knowledge I've aqquired in this role can massively benefit how I approach work. I understand the importance of giving full transparency of the work and the significance of inspecting as it progresses, as this leads to adaptation along the journey to ensure the outcome meets all the needs and requirements of the project.

Projects

Over my time at BSH, I've worked and managed various large projects that have affected multiple touchpoints, including web, social media, email, CRM tools and physical locations (showrooms and retailers).

Showroom Conversions

The previous user journey to attend a BSH showroom was extremely poor - stretching across multiple websites and an incomplete communcation cycle.

To improve on this, I worked on rolling out a new CRM tool with bespoke developments to two London Showrooms. This included creating a new journey and pages on the brand websites, designing emails to communicate with consumers, promoting the showroom to increase conversions and analysing the conversion funnel to measure ROI - all whilst communicating and working alongside various teams and stakeholders.

Digital Optimisation

I actively create new pages and journeys across each brands digital channels, to drive consumers to specific conversion points and improve upon the digital content.

Each brand has multiple campaigns and projects per year - driving consumers to new products, promotions or events. For these, I assist various teams across the business to ensure a seamless and complete journey, whilst measuring performance and finding new ways to make improvements throughout a campaigns timeline.
